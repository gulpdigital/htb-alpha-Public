Do you understand what repaying the equity loan means?

The equity loan amount you repay changes with the property price.

When the property's market value goes up or down

If your property value goes up by 5%, then the amount you owe will also go up by 5%.

If your property value goes down by 5%, the amount you owe will go down by 5%.

Example if your property price changes

Your current equity loan value is £39,600

Market value change: Amount you would owe
(excluding fees)
If the property value goes up by 5% £41,580
If the property value goes down by 5% £37,620
