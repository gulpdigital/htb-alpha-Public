Have you checked that you meet the conditions to apply for an equity loan?

You must make sure you meet the conditions required to apply for an equity loan.

 

How do I check if I meet the conditions?

To apply for an equity loan, you must:

  • be buying a new build home
  • be buying a property in England
  • be over 18 years-old
  • not own or have previously owned a residential property
  • if married, your partner cannot own or have previously owned a residential property
    • You can also use the eligibility checker on the XXX page on the GOV.UK website to check you meet the full conditions to apply for an equity loan.
