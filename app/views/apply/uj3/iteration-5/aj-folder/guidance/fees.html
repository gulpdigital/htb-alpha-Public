Equity loan fees

The loan is interest free for 5 years.

In the sixth year, you’ll be charged an interest fee of 1.75% of the original equity loan amount. The fee then increases every year by the Consumer Price Index plus 2%.

The Help to Buy service will contact you to set up these monthly fee payments. You’ll also get a statement about your loan each year.
