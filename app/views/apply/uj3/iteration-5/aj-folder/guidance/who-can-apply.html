Who can apply

You must be:

  • aged 18 years-old and over
  • buying a property in England

The home you buy must:

  • be a new build (this is a newly completed residential property)
  • be the first residential property owned (anywhere in the world) by you or any other person who is buying with you (if youâ€™re applying in England)
  • be from a registered Help to Buy housebuilder

You cannot apply if you:

  • own or have previously owned any property or land anywhere in the world. (This also includes any person buying with you)
  • are married to a person who owns or has previously owned a residential property or land anywhere in the world
