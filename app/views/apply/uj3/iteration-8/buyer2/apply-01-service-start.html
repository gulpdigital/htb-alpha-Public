Apply for a Help to Buy: Equity Loan

You have been named as the second applicant for a Help to Buy: Equity loan from the government.

What you need

To complete this application, you’ll need to check and confirm the information provided by the first applicant. This includes:

  • income and outgoings details for both applicants
  • new build property details
  • mortgage and deposit amounts
  • solicitor details

You’ll also need your proof of identity details.
