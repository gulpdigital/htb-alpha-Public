Will any other adults be living in your new home, not including the home buyers?

Tell us if any adult aged 18 years-old or over (on the date of buying your home) will be living in the property. This is because these adults will be asked to sign a certificate of occupancy.

What is a certificate of occupancy?
This is a certificate issued for adults living in the property who are not homeowners or loan applicants.
