What to do next

Check you understand what you'll pay

There are monthly fees and interest charges when you take out a loan, but you do not repay the loan in monthly instalments. Find out how to repay the equity loan.

Talk to a mortgage lender

Make sure you get a realistic idea of how much you could borrow from a mortgage lender. An independent financial advisor can help you with this.

Search for a new build home

You can buy any new build home with a Help to Buy: Equity Loan, but the homebuilder must be registered with Help to Buy. Use your regional Help to Buy agent to find a property or speak to a registered homebuilder.
