Discovery

Discovery Report

Key points

  • Lack of consistency across channels used to promote the scheme (GOV.UK, helptobuy.gov.uk, agents' websites, etc.)
  • The information currently on GOV.UK is less helpful than on helptobuy.gov.uk
  • There is no digital channel to apply for a loan
  • Almost all applications aren't even filled out by the applicant themselves - The mortgage advisor or homebuilder does it for them
