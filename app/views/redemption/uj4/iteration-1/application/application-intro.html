Apply for a quote to repay your equity loan

You can use this service:

  • when selling your home that is on Help to Buy equity loan scheme
  • to apply for a quote to repay in full

It takes approximately 15 minutes.

Start now

Before you start

You must have an offer accepted from a buyer of your home. You also need a RICS valuation report and know how much it states your home is worth.

There is a fee of £200 for application.

You’ll be asked to provide:

  • RICS valuation report
  • memorandum of sale document
  • your appointed solicitor details
  • payment details for application fee

Other ways to apply for a quote

Download the application form, fill it and send it with requirements to either:

  • repayment@homesengland.gov.uk
  • PO Box 911, Newport NP20 9PA
