Apply for a repayment quote

Use this service to apply for a full repayment quote when you sell your Help to Buy home.

It takes approximately 15 minutes.

Start now

Before you start

You will need:

  • a PDF of the RICS property valuation, or a copy to scan and upload
  • proof that a sale has been agreed such as a memorandum of sale or the final memorandum of sale
  • your solicitor's details
  • your bank details to pay the £200 application fee

Other ways to apply for a quote

Download the application form, fill in and attach the documents or email to repayment@homesengland.gov.uk or send to PO Box 911, Newport, NP10 9PA
